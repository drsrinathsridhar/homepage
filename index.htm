I am a 2nd year CS PhD student at the Max Planck Institute for Informatics. I am advised by Christian Theobalt and Antti Oulasvirta. I work in computer vision and human-computer interaction.

I completed a masters degree in EECS at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor and a bachelors degree at the College of Engineering Guindy, Anna University. I also interned at the Honda Research Institute where I worked with Victor Ng-Thow-Hing.
